About Us

Who We Are

The name Incubators is drawn from the Latin word “Incubare,” meaning “to hatch” or “to sit upon an egg in order to hatch it.” True to that meaning, The Incubators Secondary Academy is designed to nurture and develop the lives of young people. Founded on 22nd September, 2018, the Academy is located in Ufuma, Orumba North LGA, Anambra State, Nigeria, and is designed as a fully boarding school with a capacity for about 350 students.

INCUSA is a non-degree-awarding, government-approved co-educational secondary school serving boys and girls from JSS1 to SS3. As a fully boarding institution, we provide a structured environment where learning, worship, character training, and daily life are intentionally integrated — raising complete youths who look like Christ spiritually, academically, socially, and morally.

At INCUSA, we believe that education goes far beyond textbooks and examinations. We nurture the whole child — mind, body, and spirit — preparing them not just for academic success, but for a life of purpose, service, and faith.

All our staff are missionaries who are supposed to be living by faith and sacrifice. Where such are not available presently, we use volunteers and auxiliary staff, and in some few cases, paid staff — until we can get the missionaries who have received a burden for the work. Every member of the INCUSA community plays a role in shaping students into confident, God-fearing, and well-rounded individuals.

Our Core Values

What We Stand For

Christlikeness

Every aspect of school life is modelled after Christ. We teach students to reflect Christ in character, conduct, and compassion.

Academic Excellence

Rigorous preparation for WAEC, NECO, and JAMB with a well-structured curriculum from JSS1 to SS3.

Discipline

Self-control, time management, and respect for authority in a structured boarding environment. A disciplined life is a godly life.

Integrity

Honesty, truthfulness, and moral uprightness are non-negotiable standards for every member of the INCUSA community.

Service

Students are taught to serve God and others. Leadership at INCUSA means servanthood.
