About Us

Who We Are

The Incubators Secondary Academy is a fully boarding, Christian co-educational secondary school located in Ufuma, Orumba North Local Government Area of Anambra State, Nigeria. We exist to raise complete youths who look like Christ in every area of life: spiritually, academically, socially, and morally, through a blend of strong academics, intentional discipleship, and a safe, disciplined boarding environment.

The Incubators Secondary Academy is a non-degree-awarding, government-approved secondary school serving boys and girls from JSS1 to SS3. As a fully boarding institution, we provide a structured environment where learning, worship, character training, and daily life are intentionally integrated. Our community is built around teachers, disciplers, hostel staff, and support staff who see education as ministry.

At INCUSA, we believe that education goes beyond textbooks and examinations. We nurture the whole child — mind, body, and spirit — preparing them not just for academic success, but for a life of purpose, service, and faith.

Our experienced staff are passionate about both education and mentoring. Every member of our community plays a role in shaping students into confident, God-fearing, and well-rounded individuals ready to make a difference in the world.

Our Vision

To be a leading secondary school in Nigeria that raises godly, well-rounded youths equipped to excel spiritually, academically, and socially.
