Boarding Life

A Home Away from Home

Our fully boarding environment provides safety, structure, and community — where students thrive holistically.

Safe & Structured Living

Well-maintained boys’ and girls’ dormitories with dedicated hostel staff ensuring safety and comfort.

🍽️

Nutritious Meals

Three balanced meals daily plus snacks, prepared with care to fuel growing minds and bodies.

🏆

House System

Blue House and Pink House foster healthy competition, teamwork, and school spirit through inter-house activities.

⚽

Sports & Recreation

Football, table tennis, athletics, and recreational activities for physical fitness and fun.

🏥

Health & Wellness

On-campus school clinic with regular health checks to ensure every student stays healthy.

📋

Structured Daily Routine

A balanced schedule ensuring time for academics, worship, recreation, and rest.

🛡️

Safe Environment

24/7 supervision, secure premises, and a caring community where every student is known by name.
