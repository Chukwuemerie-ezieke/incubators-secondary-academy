Our Approach

Education Beyond the Classroom

At INCUSA, we believe that when it is all work and no play, it does not give the student an opportunity for total training. Education does not end all in the classroom. It revolves round what the student is exposed to, what he or she sees and knows.

  • Well-structured curriculum from JSS1 to SS3
  • WAEC, NECO & JAMB examination preparation
  • Continuous assessment and regular progress reports
  • Qualified missionary-teachers dedicated to every student
  • Holistic training — spiritual, academic, social, and moral
  • Integrated boarding life for structured study hours
